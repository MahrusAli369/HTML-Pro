Web Feed

The rel="alternate" attribute defines alternative content formats for the same page, such as Atom or RSS feeds. Browsers and feed readers can automatically detect these links to let users subscribe to your site. 

<link rel="alternate" type="application/atom+xml" href="http://example.com/feed.xml" title="Atom Feed">
<link rel="alternate" type="application/rss+xml" href="http://example.com/feed.xml" title="RSS Feed">

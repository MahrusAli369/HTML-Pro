Handling Attributes with Different Languages

The lang attribute defines the language of text content or attribute values. It can be overridden by child elements that declare a different language.

This paragraph is in English, but the next word is in German: Hallo Welt!

Voici un paragraphe en français, mais ce mot est anglais: Hello!

هذا النص بالعربية، ولكن الكلمة التالية بالإنجليزية: World!
